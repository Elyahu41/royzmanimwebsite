Settings

Methods of Calculations

  • The Amudeh Hora'ah Calendar based outside Eretz Yisrael was put together by Rabbi Leehor Dahan. It relies on the opinion of Minchat Cohen to modify the calculations based on how far north/south one is away from Netanya (which matches Bavel/Iraq in the y axis). This opinion is quoted by Rabbi David Yosef in Halacha Brurah (Chelek 15, Siman 261, halacha 13).
  • The Ohr Hachaim Calendar based in Eretz Yisrael was used by Hacham Ovadia himself. It uses elevation to account for the hill-based environment, but does not alter the timings basd on how far north/south one is located away from Netanya as a direct instruction by Hacham Ovadia (source: Rabbi Shlomo Benizri)
