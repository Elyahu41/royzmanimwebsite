Methods of Calculations

  • The Amudeh Hora'ah Calendar based outside Eretz Yisrael was put together by Rabbi Leeor Dahan. It relies on the opinion of Minchat Cohen to modify the calculations based on how far north/south one is away from Israel. This opinion is quoted by Rabbi David Yosef in Halacha Brurah (Chelek 15, Siman 261, halacha 13).
  • The Ohr Hachaim Calendar based in Eretz Yisrael was used by Hacham Ovadia himself. It uses elevation to account for the hill-based environment, but does not alter the timings basd on how far north/south one is located away from Israel based on what Rabbi Shlomo Benizri claims to be a verbal instruction by Hacham Ovadia Yosef.

Customizable Times

For Erev Yom Tov & Erev Shabbath, this field allows you to specify a personal time for Candle Lighting. The time reported for candle lighting will be the time for shkiya minus however minutes specified above:

  • 15: 3/4's of a "mil".
  • 18: The complete shiur of a "mil". Application of this is based on the "Re'em"
  • 20: The Re'em's shiur of a mil, combined with a rounding upwards for Tosefeth Shabbath.
  • 22: Different measurement of how to calculate a complete mil.
